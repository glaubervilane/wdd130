Rafting4You site

Our rafting site is located on the beautiful Snake River in Wyoming. The river winds its way through scenic canyons and offers some of the best whitewater rafting in the country.

Experience the Thrill of Rafting4You

Our rafting trips are designed to provide an unforgettable adventure for both beginners and experienced rafters.

Facilities

Our changing rooms are equipped with showers, so you can freshen up after your rafting trip.
Our snack bar offers a variety of food and drinks to keep you fueled up for your adventure.
Our picnic area is the perfect place to relax and enjoy a meal before or after your rafting trip.
We offer equipment rental for those who need it, including wetsuits, helmets, and life jackets.
