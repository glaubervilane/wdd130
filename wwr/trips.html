Perú Rafting

Join us on a thrilling adventure down the rapids of the Peruvian rivers. This trip includes three days and two nights of camping, white-water rafting, and sightseeing. All equipment and meals are provided.

Salmon River Experience

Discover the beauty of the Idaho wilderness on this five-day rafting trip on the Salmon River. Experience Class III and IV rapids, enjoy delicious meals, and relax at scenic campsites. No prior rafting experience is necessary.

Nile River Explorers

Travel to Uganda for a unique rafting adventure on the legendary Nile River. This six-day trip includes rafting on Class V rapids, exploring the Murchison Falls National Park, and cultural activities. Accommodation and meals are included.
